# GPUs Don't Grow. People Do.

People grow. GPUs do not.

Even if Quantum AI becomes reality, that principle will remain the same.

The future of humanity will not be built by investing exclusively in hardware. It will be built by investing in people.

AI does not buy food.
AI does not raise families.
AI does not strengthen local communities.
People do.

Unfortunately, many people around the world cannot afford expensive AI services. If AI is truly meant to benefit humanity, then access to it should not be limited to those who can pay the highest price.

That is why I believe AI for people should be free.

However, free software is not free to create.

Every line of code represents human effort. Behind every secure system are developers, researchers, auditors, maintainers, and countless hours of work.

There is no such thing as a free lunch.

Even when a meal is given away for free, the chef is still paid.

## Michelin recognizes excellence in cuisine:

⭐ One Star — A very good restaurant in its category.

⭐⭐ Two Stars — Excellent cooking, worth a detour.

⭐⭐⭐ Three Stars — Exceptional cuisine, worth a special journey.

If this is true for food, it is even more true for software.

Do you want cheap code?

Then treat developers as cheap labor.

Do you want secure, reliable, transparent, and world-class software?

Then invest in the people who build it.

Because GPUs don't grow.

People do.
